Sensors
=============

``example_sensor(speed, time)``
---------------------------

Parameters
^^^^^^^^^^
   * **speed** (`int`_) - The speed of the motors. Accepts values 0-100.
   * **time** (`int`_) - The amount of time for the robot to drive forward in seconds.

KIPR Functions
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
